<p>Ad blockers often block images whose filenames start with "ads". This means filenames like <code>ads1.png</code>, <code>ads1.jpg</code>, or <code>ads1.gif</code> are more likely to be blocked in browsers like Firefox and Chrome. Recently, Adblock Plus also became available for Internet Explorer.</p>

<p>To work around this, rename your image files by replacing the "ads" prefix with something else — for example, use "imgs" or "banner". Ad blockers will not match on these names, and your images will display correctly. This is a simple way to prevent your banners from being hidden on your website.</p>
