<p>I just bought a Nokia Lumia 520 last week, and this week I heard that Microsoft bought Nokia.   </p>

<p>A few months ago, my Asha 311 had some problems and stopped working. I went to the service center, and they refused to fix my issue. I had no other option. In the end, I bought a brand-new Lumia 520 phone.</p>

<p>I just read the news that Microsoft bought Nokia. I don't know what will happen to the future of the phone. Maybe Microsoft will put their own logo on phones that previously had "Nokia" printed on them.</p>
