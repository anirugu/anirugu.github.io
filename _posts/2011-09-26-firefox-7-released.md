I came across an article on [ghacks.net](http://www.ghacks.net/2011/09/27/mozilla-firefox-7-released/) about Firefox 7, Mozilla's upcoming release. At the time of writing, the official Mozilla website still showed version 6.0.2, meaning Firefox 7 hadn't been officially announced yet—but it was expected to go live within the next 24 hours.

If you want to grab the build early before the official announcement, you can download it directly from Mozilla's FTP server:

- **Windows:** [ftp://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/releases/7.0/win32/](ftp://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/releases/7.0/win32/)
- **All platforms:** [ftp://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/releases/7.0/](ftp://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/releases/7.0/)
